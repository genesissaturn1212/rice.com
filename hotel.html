Introducting: Hotel Tugu Bali

Sitting next to the sands and sea of Echo Beach in Canggu, the boutique Hotel Tugu Bali really appeals to one’s aesthetic senses. Packed with eye-catching ornamental objects, antiques, and works of art, the property creates the perfect balance between bold colours, authentic Balinese designs, and modern features. In addition to the main swimming pool, some rooms also have private mini pools!

Introducing: Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak

Part of Intercontinental Hotels Group’s Hotel Indigo brand, the large and stylish Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach boasts beautiful local designs, rich colours, interesting décor, and exquisite furnishings. Located right next to the sands of Seminyak Beach, the property enjoys a lively location. Shopping, dining, sightseeing, diverse activities, and nightlife are right at your doorstep. Guests can even explore the surrounding area with bicycles that are available for rental at the hotel! There’s also plenty to keep visitors entertained without even needing to set foot out of the resort.
